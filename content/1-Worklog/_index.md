**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period from 04/05/2026 - 25/07/2026) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1 (04/05/2026 - 08/05/2026):** [Getting Started & Core AWS Services (Explore AWS Services - Part 1)](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2 (11/05/2026 - 15/05/2026):** [Storage, Database & Load Balancing (Explore AWS Services - Part 2)](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3 (18/05/2026 - 22/05/2026):** [Windows Workloads & Infrastructure Migration (Migrate to AWS)](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4 (25/05/2026 - 29/05/2026):** [System Optimization - Operations & Security (Optimizing AWS - Operations & Security)](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5 (01/06/2026 - 05/06/2026):** [Reliability, Cost Management & Container Services (Reliability, Cost & Containers)](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6 (08/06/2026 - 12/06/2026):** [Advanced Containers (EKS) and Application Modernization (Modernize - DevAx)](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7 (15/06/2026 - 19/06/2026):** [Application Modernization - Serverless (Book Store & DMS Series)](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8 (22/06/2026 - 26/06/2026):** [Data Analytics & Artificial Intelligence (AI/ML Services)](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9 (29/06/2026 - 03/07/2026):** [Final Project - Phase 1: Ideation, Architecture Design & Initialization](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10 (06/07/2026 - 10/07/2026):** [Final Project - Phase 2: Application Development & Infrastructure Deployment](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11 (13/07/2026 - 17/07/2026):** [Final Project - Phase 3: CI/CD Integration & System Optimization](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12 (20/07/2026 - 25/07/2026):** [Final Project - Phase 4: Testing, Documentation & Final Defense](1.12-week12/)
